ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Bijay Shrestha. I am a Computer Engineer and Web Developer. When I'm not glued to the computer screen, I mostly sleep.

They reflect the personality of an individual and I firmly believe in the power of individuality and the freedom it brings. I stand by my beliefs and have a strong work ethic.

I am diligent, attentive to details and complete my work in time. While three isn't necessarily my favourite number, there are three things I would like to add: practice what you preach; quality over quantity; and climate change is real.
